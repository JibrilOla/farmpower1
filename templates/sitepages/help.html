How to Recharge

1. Navigate to your Dashboard, then click on Recharge Button

2. Input amount and your E-mail address. (take notes of the warnings)

3. Confirm your recharge

4. Select Payment Method

5. Make Payment via your selected method and wait for confirmation.

How to Withdraw

1. Navigate to Dashboard and click the Withdraw Button.

2. Input all required fields correctly

3. Proceed and wait for your withdrawal to be processed

How to Purchase Machine

1. Navigate to Machines

2. Select Machine to purchase and confirm your purchase

How to Recieve Income

1. Navigate to Dashbord and click on 'My machines'.

2. Recieve income from all your Machines.

Invitation Rules
